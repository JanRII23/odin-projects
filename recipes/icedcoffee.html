Iced Coffee

Description

The best Iced Coffee beverage served is fast and easy. It may be prepared either by brewing coffee in the normal way and then serving it over ice or in cold milk, or by brewing the coffee cold. In hot brewing, sweeteners and flavoring may be added before cooling, as they dissolve faster. Iced coffee can also be sweetened with pre-dissolved sugar in water.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 - cup warm water
  • 2 - teaspoons instant coffee granules
  • 1 - tray ice cubes
  • 1/2 (5 ounce) - can sweetened condensed milk
  • 1/2 - cup milk
  • 1 - tablespoon chocolate syrup

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, stir together the water and instant coffee. In a blender, combine ice cubes, coffee mixture, milk, sweetened condensed milk and chocolate syrup. Blend until smooth. Pour into glasses and serve.
